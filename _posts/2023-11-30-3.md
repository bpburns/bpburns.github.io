## "Amish" Baked Oatmeal

A favorite of my kids' for many years, this recipe is foolproof.

[Single Batch](/recipes/amish-baked-oatmeal.html)

### Ingredients
- 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 2 large eggs
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg or cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 3 cups oatmeal, regular or quick-cooking

### Directions
1. Melt butter.
2. Grease 1 1/2 qt. baking dish
3. Drop in eggs and beat well
4. Add brown sugar, baking powder, vanilla, nutmeg or cinnamon, and salt. Mix well. No Lumps.
5. Whisk in butter and both measures of milk.
6. Add oats.
7. Stir well.
8. Refirgerate overnight.
9. Bake uncovered at 350 F for 35-45 minutes, until set in middle.
10. Serve hot with warm milk poured over.
