ingredients:
  - 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  - 2 large eggs
  - 3/4 cup brown sugar
  - 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  - 1 teaspoon nutmeg or cinnamon
  - 1/4 teaspoon salt
  - 1 cup milk
  - 2 tablespoons milk
  - 3 cups oatmeal, regular or quick-cooking
cuisine: American
date: 2023-12-01
instructions:
  - Melt butter.
  - Grease 1 1/2 qt. baking dish
  - Drop in eggs and beat well
  - Add brown sugar, baking powder, vanilla, nutmeg or cinnamon, and salt. Mix well. No Lumps.
  - Whisk in butter and both measures of milk.
  - Add oats.
  - Stir well.
  - Refirgerate overnight.
  - Bake uncovered at 350 F for 35-45 minutes, until set in middle.
  - Serve hot with warm milk poured over.
