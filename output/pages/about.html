About Me

My name is Brian Barefoot Burns. No, it’s not a Native American name. No, “Barefoot” is not my nickname, and it does not belong in quotes between “Brian” and “Burns” My unmarried name is Burns. My wife’s maiden name is Barefoot. When we got married, she didn’t want to completely give up her cool last name. We also decided that if we had children, we wanted every member of the family to share the same last name. Our marriage license was issued in Multnomah County, Oregon. One of the prescribed options for us was to combine our unmarried last names into a new, two-word last name, which we chose. In hindsight, it might have caused less confusion for everyone if we had chosen the hyphenated option, but here we are. We now have two children, both named Barefoot Burns.
